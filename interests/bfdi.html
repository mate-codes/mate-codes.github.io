What is BFDI?

Battle for Dream Island (BFDI for short) is an American reality TV viewer-voting animated web series created by the Huang Twins named Michael Huang and Cary Huang, who are most known by their YouTube channel name, jacknjellify. The series is about a competition between inanimate, anthropomorphic objects. It premiered on January 1, 2010, and is currently airing its fifth season. It is also airing its second season, following its revival. The entire series is uploaded on YouTube, on the channel jacknjellify.

Source: Battle for Dream Island Wiki
